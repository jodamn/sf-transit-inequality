Income by MBTA Station Around Boston

A recent feature in The New Yorker looked at New York City subway stops and resident incomes. Two developers in San Francisco had the idea did some work to map incomes by transit station in the Bay Area. We've extended their code to map income by MBTA stations around Boston.

Methodology

Stop and route information came from the MBTA. Income data was gathered from the US Census API. We used the incomes from the census tract where each stop is located.
